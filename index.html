About Us

Best Health Care For You!

Hind Medi Tour is the most popular and trusted medical assistance company in India. It is one of leading medical tourism companies in India. The company serves a large number of international patients from all over the world and helps them get the most affordable and high-quality treatment in India every year.

We are a very patient-centric company. We at Hind Medi Tour strive hard to provide hassle free services, complete guidance and assistance in and outside the hospital. We have successfully served patients from all over the world, especially from Arab and African countries such as Iraq, Yemen, Oman, Sudan, UAE, Kuwait, Zimbabwe and Kenya etc.

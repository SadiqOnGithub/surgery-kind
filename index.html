Our Services

We provide following services for international patients to get affordable and hassle-free treatment in India

Free Opinion and Cost Estimate

If you are looking for best treatment in India, you are at right place. We can provide you with free treatment plan and cost estimate from leading hospital in India.

Free Visa Invitation Latter & Visa Assistance

Medical visa is required to get treatment in India. Visa invitation latter will help you get medical visa from the Indian embassy in your country.

Free Airport Pick-up & Drop

We provide free pick up and drop services for international patients. When you arrive at Airport one of our team members will be present at the Airport to receive you.

Free Language Interpreter

English is the main communication language in Indian private hospitals. If you are not sound enough in English, you can request us for an interpreter.

Free Local Guidance & Assistance

A dedicated member of our patient support team will be always there to guide and assist you in all the things related to your treatment inside and outside the hospital.

Assistance in Foreign Exchange

We assist our patients and their attendants in foreign currency exchange and help them get the best rate.
